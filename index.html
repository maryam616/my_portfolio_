ABOUT ME

Hi, I amm an 18-year-old self-taught learner from Kabul, Afghanistan, with a deep passion for creativity, technology, and empowering others through education. Since I couldn’t attend traditional school due to the Taliban regime, I took charge of my own learning—exploring HTML, CSS, and digital tools, while also teaching kids online to make learning fun and accessible. I am m especially passionate about building things that have real meaning, whether it is designing beautiful websites, creating educational content, or dreaming up ways to solve problems in my community. I believe that resilience, curiosity, and compassion are my superpowers. My goal is to keep learning, keep growing, and use tech to make the world a little kinder and a little smarter.
